Account Deletion Process

If you wish to delete your account, follow these simple steps:

  1. Go to the Profile Section: Open the app and navigate to the Profile section.
  2. Access the Account Settings: Tap on the Account section.
  3. Delete Account Option: You’ll see an option to Delete Account. Click this to proceed.
  4. Confirmation Popup: A confirmation popup will appear asking if you’re sure you want to delete your account. Click Yes to permanently delete your account.

Data Deletion Timeline

Once your account is deleted, all personal data (such as username, date of birth, phone number, state, city, etc.) will be permanently removed from our system. Please note that this process may take 24 to 48 hours.
